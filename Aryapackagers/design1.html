Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is the minimum order quantity for custom product boxes?

    The minimum order quantity is 100 pieces. You can get custom boxes at wholesale rates by increasing your order quantity.

  • How long does production take?

    The production process begins as soon as we receive your order confirmation and everything is completed according to your specifications. You can expect your order to be manufactured within a week. In the case of special or bulk orders, setup and production may take more time.

  • How long does shipping take?

    Our top priority is delivering your order on time, as we are one of the top custom packaging suppliers. After production, it takes 5 to 7 business days for your shipment to arrive at your destination once it's loaded.

  • Do you offer free samples?

    Yes, we provide free samples. The packaging sample is available only on demand, and you must pay shipping charges.

  • Do you charge for plates and dies? Are there any hidden costs?

    No, we do not charge for plates or dies. Also, there are no hidden charges.

  • How much do custom boxes cost?

    You can request a quote for any box style you need by clicking "Get a Quote" on this page. Choose the box style, material, size, and quantity you want. You will get an instant pricing update via e-mail once you make your selections.

  • Will I receive proof before my order is printed?

    Yes, we will provide digital proof to you of your custom packaging boxes before we begin production. Once your order is placed, you will receive it via e-mail. Also, you can review your online proof.

  • Can you produce more than 2,000 units?

    Sure, let us know what you need, and we will assist you with your project in every way we can. Feel free and contact us to get cheap custom boxes.

  • What packaging products can I order from Blue Box?

    Bluebox Packaging is one of the leading custom box manufacturers. We offer a wide range of product boxes that meet the needs of you and your products. Our variety of box styles is perfect for retail packaging or e-commerce deliveries.

  • Can you print inside the packaging boxes?

    It is absolutely memorable to see inside printed packaging. The branded boxes we create can be printed both inside and outside.

  • Do you offer custom inserts?

    Yes, we also offer custom inserts. Our team will help you through the process when you contact us with your project details.

  • What can I do to reduce the price per unit?

    As box quantities increase, pricing becomes more cost-efficient, so the more you order, the lower your per-unit price will be. The price of your box is generally determined by its dimensions, box style, ink coverage, box material, and quantity. Among the leading custom box manufacturers, BBP offers competitive prices on custom-printed boxes. We can beat any verified quote you find anywhere. It's important to us to maintain a reputation as one of the most cost-effective providers of custom packaging printing.

  • What is your turnaround time?

    The turnaround time varies depending on the order, but usually, it is between 5 to 7 business days. The turnaround times do not include transit time (5 - 7 business days, depending on your location). There may be an additional few days of setup and production time for bulk orders. It may take a longer time for some orders to be processed during extremely busy periods.

  • Is my art printable?

    Our professional team will check your custom box design for any technical issues (blurriness, artwork resolution, bleeds, splits, and thin lines) before proofing for your approval. Please feel free to contact our prepress department if having any questions regarding printing concerns that have been noted.

  • Do you provide instant quotes?

    Yes, an instant quote is provided once you select the size, material, and quantity of your order.
